Conditional Reasoning

In the following tasks, you will be presented with two sentences for which you need to assume that they are true.

Your task is then to determine what follows necessarily (meaning that it is guaranteed to be true) from both sentences together. For that, you will be presented with three options to choose from.

Consider the following example:

What, if anything, follows?

Select the option that necessarily follows from the information above. If neither option follows, select Neither option follows.

After selecting your answer, click on Continue to proceed with the next task.
