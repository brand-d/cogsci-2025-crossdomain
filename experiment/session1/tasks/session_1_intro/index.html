Welcome to the first session!

Please make sure you can complete the whole experiment without interruption. Please avoid any distractions (i.e., TV, music, smartphones).

Please do not use a smartphone or tablet to participate in this study, as it is optimized for the use of a desktop or laptop. If possible, maximize the browser window to make sure you see the tasks without having to scroll. Also, do not use the back-button of your browser or refresh this page, as it might render your experiment invalid.

With this study, we aim at advancing our understanding of human reasoning. Therefore, it is important that you use your memory and mental operations for the following tasks. Please do not use external tools (like pen and paper).
